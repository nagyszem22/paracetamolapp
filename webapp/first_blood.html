Warning!

The time difference between the time when the patient ingested the paracetamol dose and the time when the blood sample was taken is smaller than 4 hours.

At under 4 hours the Paracetamol Plasma Levels may not reliable enough to determine treatment. It is advised to wait until the time gap reaches 4 hours and create a new form then.

You can choose to proceed to treatment anyway or just input the final blood tests and submit the form without the treatments.
